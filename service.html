Packing

1. 1kg/aluminium foil bag, with two plastic bags inside.
2. 25kg/fiber drum, with two plastic bags inside. Size: ID42cm×H52cm, 0.08m3/Drum; Gross Weight: 28kg.



 

Delivery

1. ≤50kg, Express delivery recommended, usually called as DDU service;
