Curcumin is a chemical component extracted from the rhizome of some plants in the family Zingiberaceae and Araceae. In the above-mentioned several natural plants, Curcumin contains about 3%-6%.Curcumin is a diketone pigment that is rare in the plant kingdom. Curcumin is an orange-yellow crystalline powder with a slightly bitter taste and not soluble in water.
