Almond flour is a gluten-free nut flour that should consist of nothing but ground blanched (no skin) almonds.

It’s often used as a low-carb substitute for wheat flour in bread and other baked goods.

 

Almond flour is rich in fiber, phosphorus, iron, calcium, vitamin B17 and unsaturated fatty acids and other important nutrients.
